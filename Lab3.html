Agents

There are currently 4 Roles in valorant which are Duelist who are responsible for entrying site, and be the first to engage with the enemy. Controllers, are responsible for smoking the map, to gain map control. Initiators help gather enemy informations, and help teammates on entering sites.Lastly Sentinel gathers intel on where the enemy are, anchoring site to prevent enemy pushes, and watch out for any flankers.
